## Why can I not see commands?

Check officer permissions and guild command registration status.

## Why did `/vote` not post?

Most often the officer channel is not configured or not writable by the bot.

## Why do role updates fail?

The bot role is below the target role in hierarchy or lacks manage roles permission.

## Why did reminders skip?

Either settings are missing or schedule/threshold values are not configured.
