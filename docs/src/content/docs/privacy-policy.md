_Last updated: May 24, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how Gatekeeper collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Gatekeeper documentation site and Discord bot.

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- The Gatekeeper documentation website.
- The Gatekeeper Discord application (bot).

## Information we collect

Gatekeeper may process the following categories of data:

- Discord server IDs and channel IDs needed to store guild-specific settings.
- Discord user IDs needed to attribute commands, trial actions, and feedback.
- Trial workflow records such as trial status, summaries, votes, and reminders.
- Operational metadata and logs needed to diagnose issues and keep the service reliable.

Gatekeeper is not designed to collect sensitive personal data.

## How we use information

We use data to:

- Provide and operate trial workflow features.
- Maintain guild-specific configuration and automation.
- Improve reliability, detect abuse, and troubleshoot errors.

## Data sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Data may be shared only when necessary with infrastructure providers that host and operate Gatekeeper, or when required by law.

## Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide service functionality, maintain service integrity, and comply with legal obligations.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect stored data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Your choices

Server administrators can remove Gatekeeper from a server at any time. If you need help with data-related requests, contact us through our support channel.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open a support request at:

- https://github.com/GatekeeperInc/gatekeeper/issues
